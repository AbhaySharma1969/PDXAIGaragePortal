Prompt Workshop: make AI useful.

A good prompt is not magic. It is a clear instruction with role, task, context, constraints, format, and quality bar.

Weak prompt

Help me with Spanish.

Too vague. AI has to guess what kind of help you need.

Strong prompt

Act as my Spanish 1–2 coach. Quiz me on food vocabulary one question at a time. Give hints before answers. Correct my spelling gently. At the end, list my weak words.

Clear role, task, topic, rules, and output.

Prompt formula

  • Role
  • Task
  • Context
  • Constraints
  • Format
  • Quality bar
