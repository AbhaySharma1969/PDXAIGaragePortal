Lab 4

Ethics + Safety: use AI without losing your judgment.

The goal is to use AI as a coach and builder, not as a shortcut that replaces thinking.

Good uses

  • Explain a topic in simpler language.
  • Quiz me before showing answers.
  • Help organize notes.
  • Give hints and similar examples.
  • Critique my draft and suggest improvements.

Bad uses

  • Copying answers and pretending they are yours.
  • Submitting AI writing without understanding it.
  • Pasting private information into tools.
  • Making accusations based only on AI output.
  • Using AI to avoid practice.

The 7 Garage AI rules

  1. Use AI to learn, not to fake learning.
  2. Ask for hints before answers.
  3. Check facts before using them.
  4. Do not paste private information.
  5. Label claims, opinions, and facts.
  6. Keep your own voice in final work.
  7. Every AI output is a draft until a human improves it.
