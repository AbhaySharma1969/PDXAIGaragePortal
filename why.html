Why we exist

Teenagers already have AI. They need judgment.

PDX AI Garage began as a father-son summer project. The goal was not to teach another tool. The goal was to help a teenager become a more capable builder in an AI-powered world.

The problem

Most teens can open an AI chatbot. That does not mean they know when to trust it, how to use it responsibly, how to turn ideas into artifacts, or how to explain their thinking.

Random videos teach fragments. PDX AI Garage creates a structured apprenticeship.

Our promise

Builders leave with confidence, judgment, and a growing portfolio. They learn AI by solving interesting problems, building real artifacts, and improving their own work.

What makes this different

Apprenticeship, not course

Mentor-guided missions with artifacts and reflections.

Human advantage

Communication, questioning, ethics, leadership, and judgment are built into every journey.
