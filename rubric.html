How XP is earned

  • Complete the build: 40–60 XP
  • Test and improve it: +10 XP
  • Explain what failed: +10 XP
  • Demo clearly: +20 XP
  • Help improve the portal: +15 XP

Quality Rubric

CategoryGreatNeeds Work
Prompt qualityClear role, task, context, constraints, and output format.Vague request or no success criteria.
Human judgmentStudent checks the answer, asks follow-ups, and improves it.Copies AI output without thinking.
EvidenceClaims are labeled, verified, or marked uncertain.AI statements are treated as automatically true.
Build artifactThere is a usable prompt, memo, tracker, page, or demo.Only discussion, no output.
ReflectionStudent explains what worked, what failed, and what to improve.No learning note or vague “it was good.”
