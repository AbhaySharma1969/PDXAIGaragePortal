Build a math tutor that asks questions and gives hints before final answers.

Coach prompt

Act as my algebra tutor. Do not give the final answer first. Ask me what step I would try. If I am stuck, give one hint. If I make a mistake, explain it simply and let me try again. Use a similar example before solving my exact problem.

Session output

By the end of this session, create:

  • Artifact: Algebra tutor prompt and one corrected mistake example.
  • Proof: one screenshot, note, prompt, table, or mini-demo
  • Reflection: what worked, what failed, and what version 2 should improve
School Tools rule: A school-safe AI tutor should build understanding, not replace the thinking step.
