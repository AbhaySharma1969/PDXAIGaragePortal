Teaching Guide

Guide without taking over.

Use a 45–55 minute session: set the mission, learn/check, practice with AI, build artifact, reflect.

Mentor questions

Can you explain the idea without reading?What did AI help with?What did AI get wrong or vague?What evidence or test did you use?What would version 2 improve?

Rubric

1 Started: read/copied2 Practiced: rough artifact3 Builder: tested and improved4 Founder: can teach, show limits, and propose v2
