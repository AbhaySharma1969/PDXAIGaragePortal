Lab 3

Verification Lab: trust, but check.

AI can sound confident even when it is wrong. This lab teaches Sahil to label claims before believing them.

Green

Safe general advice or explanation that does not depend on a precise fact.

Yellow

Needs checking: dates, prices, names, laws, current events, medical/legal/financial facts.

Red

Risky: unsupported claims, accusations, private information, or answers that could cause harm.

Claim checker workspace

Paste an AI answer or claim. The portal will turn it into a verification checklist.

Your verification checklist will appear here.

Verification prompt

Break this answer into individual claims. Mark each claim as Green, Yellow, or Red. For every Yellow or Red claim, tell me exactly what source or evidence would be needed before trusting it. Do not make new claims.
