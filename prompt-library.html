Reusable AI Prompts

Prompt library for the Garage.

These are starter prompts. Sahil should edit them, test them, and improve them as part of the build.

Practice in Prompt WorkshopOpen Verification Lab
Universal

The Useful Answer Prompt

Act as a helpful coach. My goal is [goal]. My current level is [beginner/intermediate]. Give me a clear answer, but first ask up to 3 questions if you need context. Use simple language, give examples, and end with one action I can take today.
School

Teach Me, Don’t Cheat Prompt

Help me learn this without just giving me the answer. Ask me one question at a time. Give hints before solutions. When I make a mistake, explain the mistake simply and let me try again.
Investigation

Facts vs Claims Prompt

Review the notes below. Create a table with three columns: verified facts, claims/allegations, and opinions/emotional language. Mark anything uncertain. Do not make conclusions beyond the evidence.
Sports

Training Plan Prompt

Create a realistic 7-day training plan for [basketball/golf]. I have [time] per day. My goal is [goal]. Include warmup, drill, reflection question, and one measurable target.
Product

Prototype Critic Prompt

Act as a product tester for a teen AI learning portal. Review this idea/page. Tell me what is clear, what is boring, what is confusing, and what one change would make it more useful.
Showcase

Demo Story Prompt

Help me create a 3-minute demo script with this structure: problem, what I built, how AI helped, what went wrong, what I learned, and what I would improve next.

Week 3 school prompts

These are the safest school-use prompts because they force AI to teach, quiz, and explain instead of just giving answers.

Spanish

Spanish Vocabulary Coach Prompt

Act as my Spanish 1–2 coach. Quiz me one word at a time. Give hints before answers. Correct spelling and grammar gently. Track my weak words and make a 10-minute practice plan.
Algebra

Algebra Tutor Prompt

Do not give me the final answer first. Ask me what step I would try. Give hints if I am stuck. Explain mistakes simply and let me try again. Use a similar example before solving my exact problem.

Week 4–6 build prompts

Use these for sports systems, mini-app prototypes, and the final showcase sprint.

Sports

Practice Reflection Prompt

Create a practice reflection from these notes. Find patterns, one likely improvement target, and one question I should ask a coach. Do not make medical or expert claims.
Product

Decision Matrix Prompt

Create a weighted decision matrix for these options: [options]. Criteria: [criteria]. Score each option, explain the winner, and list missing information before making a final recommendation.
Prototype

Mini-App Builder Prompt

Help me turn this idea into a simple prototype. Define the user, problem, inputs, output, scoring logic, and one test case.
Showcase

Final Demo Reflection Prompt

Create a final reflection with what I built, three AI skills I learned, one mistake I corrected, what I am proudest of, and what I would build next.
