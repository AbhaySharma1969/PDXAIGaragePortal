Mission: Founder Friday: Demo What Worked

Turn the first week into a mini demo: what we built, what worked, what failed, and what should change next.

Time

55 min

Keep it focused and practical.

AI Skill

Reflection + demo

One skill, used immediately.

Output

Week 1 demo

Something visible by the end.

Proof

Screenshot + log

Evidence of progress.

Mission timeline

0–10

Review artifacts

Look at the founder profile, prompt card, trust checklist, and assistant setup.

10–25

Pick the best

Choose the most useful artifact and explain why it helped.

25–40

Build demo story

Create a short problem-build-result-learning script.

40–50

Record or rehearse

Do a 2-minute demo for the family or camera.

XP log

Choose one change for Week 2.

Starter prompt

Create a 2-minute demo script from these notes: [paste notes]. Use this structure: what I tried, what I built, what worked, what failed, and what I want to improve next.

Done means done

  • Week 1 demo exists.
  • One screenshot or note is saved.
  • One thing that worked is recorded.
  • One thing to improve is recorded.
  • 60 XP is logged.
