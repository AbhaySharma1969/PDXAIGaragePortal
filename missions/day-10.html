Day 10 · 75 XP

Mission: Founder Ethics Brief

Finish Week 2 by writing a responsible business recommendation: what a founder, franchise, or marketplace should do next.

Time

55 min

Keep it focused and practical.

AI Skill

Business ethics

One skill, used immediately.

Output

Recommendation brief

Something visible by the end.

Proof

Screenshot + log

Evidence of progress.

Mission timeline

0–10

List stakeholders

Identify who is affected: customers, owners, employees, brand, community, and online audience.

10–25

List risks

Separate reputation risk, legal risk, customer trust risk, and ethical risk.

25–40

Draft recommendation

Recommend 3 practical next steps a responsible business should take.

40–50

Founder review

Check whether the recommendation is fair, evidence-based, and not overconfident.

50–55

XP log

Record the top business lesson from the case.

Starter prompt

Act as a fair business ethics advisor. Based only on the case file below, identify stakeholders, risks, responsibilities, and 3 recommended next steps. Do not assume guilt. Mark what is known, unknown, and recommended.

Done means done

  • Recommendation brief exists.
  • One screenshot or note is saved.
  • One thing that worked is recorded.
  • One thing to improve is recorded.
  • 75 XP is logged.
