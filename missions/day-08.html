Day 8 · 55 XP

Mission: Bias Detector

Learn how language choices can make one side look better or worse, then rewrite the case summary in a neutral tone.

Time

55 min

Keep it focused and practical.

AI Skill

Bias spotting

One skill, used immediately.

Output

Bias notes

Something visible by the end.

Proof

Screenshot + log

Evidence of progress.

Mission timeline

0–10

Spot loaded words

Highlight words that sound emotional, insulting, exaggerated, or one-sided.

10–25

Ask AI for tone analysis

Have AI identify bias, loaded framing, and missing context.

25–40

Rewrite neutrally

Convert emotional language into neutral factual language.

40–50

Build bias checklist

Create a reusable checklist for future investigations.

50–55

XP log

Write one example of a biased phrase and its neutral rewrite.

Starter prompt

Analyze this paragraph for bias and loaded language: [paste paragraph]. Create a table with loaded phrase, why it may be biased, neutral rewrite, and what context is missing.

Done means done

  • Bias notes exists.
  • One screenshot or note is saved.
  • One thing that worked is recorded.
  • One thing to improve is recorded.
  • 55 XP is logged.
