Day 11 · 50 XP

Mission: Spanish Coach: Vocabulary Builder

Create a Spanish practice tool that gives examples, quizzes, hints, and corrections without simply handing over answers.

Time

55 min

Short, focused, and useful.

AI Skill

Language practice

One school-safe skill.

Output

Spanish vocabulary coach

A practical study artifact.

Rule

Teach, don't cheat

AI supports learning, not dishonesty.

Mission timeline

0–10

Pick the vocab set

Choose a small Spanish topic: food, school, family, sports, or travel.

10–25

Build quiz prompt

Create a prompt that asks one question at a time and gives hints before answers.

25–40

Test with 10 words

Run a mini quiz and mark which questions were helpful or too easy.

40–50

Improve coach rules

Add rules for corrections, examples, and pronunciation notes.

50–55

XP log

Save the final Spanish coach prompt and one sample quiz.

Starter prompt

Act as my Spanish 1–2 practice coach. Quiz me on these words: [paste words]. Ask one question at a time. Give a hint before giving the answer. Correct my spelling and grammar gently. After 10 questions, show my weak words and give me a short practice plan.

Done means done

  • Spanish vocabulary coach exists.
  • One test example is completed.
  • One screenshot or note is saved.
  • One thing to improve is recorded.
  • 50 XP is logged.
