Day 7 · 60 XP

Mission: Build a Neutral Timeline

Use AI to arrange public information into a clean timeline while marking missing dates, uncertain events, and unsupported claims.

Time

55 min

Keep it focused and practical.

AI Skill

Timeline building

One skill, used immediately.

Output

Timeline v1

Something visible by the end.

Proof

Screenshot + log

Evidence of progress.

Mission timeline

0–10

Collect timeline notes

Gather events, dates, posts, articles, statements, or milestones.

10–25

Generate timeline v1

Ask AI to put events in order and mark anything missing a date.

25–40

Challenge the timeline

Look for assumptions, skipped steps, or events that need a source.

40–50

Create timeline v2

Rewrite the timeline in neutral language with uncertainty labels.

50–55

XP log

Record the most important missing fact or date.

Starter prompt

Turn these notes into a neutral timeline. Use columns for date, event, source, confidence level, and unanswered question. If a date is missing, write “date unknown.” Do not fill gaps with guesses.

Done means done

  • Timeline v1 exists.
  • One screenshot or note is saved.
  • One thing that worked is recorded.
  • One thing to improve is recorded.
  • 60 XP is logged.
