Signature Mission

The LEGO Case File

A real-world AI investigation module using a high-interest LEGO business controversy as the hook.

Mission objective

Use AI to build a careful, fair, evidence-based case file. Separate facts from allegations, identify missing information, and evaluate business ethics.

AI skills learned

Fact checkingTimeline buildingBias detectionPrompt designEthics
StepBuild TaskOutput

Important standard

This mission should not become online gossip. The goal is to learn how AI can help organize public information responsibly and avoid unfair conclusions.
