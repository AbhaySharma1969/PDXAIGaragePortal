Signature Week 2 Module

The LEGO Case File

A real-world AI investigation module using a high-interest LEGO business controversy as the hook. The learning goal is careful reasoning, not online gossip.

Start Day 6Open Evidence Template

Mission objective

Use AI to build a careful, fair, evidence-based case file. Separate facts from allegations, identify missing information, and evaluate business ethics.

AI skills learned

Fact checkingTimeline buildingBias detectionPrompt designEthics

Important standard

This mission should not become online gossip. The goal is to learn how AI can organize public information responsibly and avoid unfair conclusions.
