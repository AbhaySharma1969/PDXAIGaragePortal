Glossary

AI words in plain English.

AIA system that predicts, decides, or generates from patterns.
Machine LearningAI that learns patterns from examples.
ModelThe trained system that produces outputs.
Training DataExamples used to teach a model.
Test DataExamples used to check a model.
LabelThe category or answer attached to examples.
BiasSkewed or unfair patterns in data, design, or output.
HallucinationA confident AI output that is false or unsupported.
PromptThe design brief you give AI.
AuditA structured check for risk, error, bias, and evidence.
AgentAn AI system that can pursue goals using tools.
CapstoneA final project that proves learning.
