# Playbook Chapter 1 — Why We Exist

PDX AI Garage exists because teenagers already have access to powerful AI tools, but access is not the same as readiness.

A teenager can ask AI for an answer. That does not mean they know how to judge the answer, improve it, explain it, or turn it into something useful.

We believe teenagers are capable of far more than most educational systems expect. Our job is not to lower the bar. Our job is to build a bridge that helps them reach it.

## The founding belief

Teenagers do not dream about learning. They dream about becoming.

PDX AI Garage helps them become builders.

## The Garage promise

Every builder should leave with:
- Better questions
- Better judgment
- Real artifacts
- Confidence to build
- A habit of improving Version 1

## What we are not

We are not a prompt library.
We are not a video course.
We are not a quiz platform.
We are not trying to replace school.

## What we are

We are a project-based AI apprenticeship for teen builders.

AI is the medium.
Human capability is the mission.
