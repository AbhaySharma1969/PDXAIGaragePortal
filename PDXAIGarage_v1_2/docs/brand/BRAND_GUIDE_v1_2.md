# PDX AI Garage Brand Guide v1.2

## Brand idea
A modern garage for young builders learning to create in an AI-powered world.

## Tagline
Build your future. Powered by AI.

## Internal motto
Learn. Build. Question. Improve.

## Language
Use:
- Builder
- Journey
- Mission
- Passport
- Notebook
- District
- Studio
- Lab
- Artifact
- Version 2

Avoid:
- Student
- Homework
- Lesson
- Quiz
- Course module
- LMS

## Voice
Warm, confident, practical, human, slightly adventurous.

## Tone benchmark
Make a 15-year-old curious and make a parent trust us.

## Visual direction
Modern workshop, not sci-fi. Warm, clean, ambitious, product-quality.
