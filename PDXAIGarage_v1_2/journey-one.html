Journey One · AI Detective Bureau

The Case of the Confident AI.

Your AI partner has produced a report that sounds convincing. Your job is to find what is verified, what is assumed, and what might be wrong.

Mission brief

A local family is trying to understand a confusing online story about collectibles, trust, business responsibility, and missing evidence. People online are reacting emotionally. Your job is not to take sides. Your job is to build a fair case file.

Do not accuse people. Do not repeat rumors as facts. Builders verify before believing.

What you will build

  • Claim vs. evidence table
  • Timeline of known events
  • Questions still unanswered
  • AI reliability scorecard
  • One-page case summary

Detective workflow

1

Ask AI for a summary

Then mark every claim as verified, unverified, opinion, or missing context.

2

Build the evidence board

What do we know? How do we know it? What source supports it?

3

Find the weak spots

Where did AI sound confident without enough evidence?

4

Write the fair summary

Explain the situation without hype, accusation, or drama.
