Final Demo Roadmap

Founder Showcase: what we built and learned.

The final showcase is the proof that this was not just AI talk. It is a short demo of useful artifacts, responsible AI judgment, and product thinking.

1. Best AI prompt

Show the prompt that produced the most useful result and explain why it worked.

2. LEGO Case File

Show facts, claims, timeline, bias notes, and the ethics recommendation.

3. School helper

Demo the Spanish or algebra tool that teaches instead of cheats.

4. Sports or e-bike tool

Show a real training plan, decision matrix, or tracker.
