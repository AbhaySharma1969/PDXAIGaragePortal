6-Week AI Builder Path

A practical AI literacy curriculum disguised as a product-building sprint. The goal is not to “study AI.” The goal is to use AI responsibly to build useful things.

WeekThemeAI SkillsFounder Build
1AI basics + prompt powerPrompting, role/context/format, verification, AI limitsPrompt Playbook + Garage identity
2AI investigationFact vs claim sorting, timeline creation, bias detectionLEGO Case File: fact timeline, claims map, ethics memo
3AI for schoolTutoring prompts, quiz design, study guide creation, academic honestySpanish practice bot + algebra helper that teaches, not cheats
4AI for sportsPlanning, reflection, habit tracking, performance notesGolf/basketball training plan and reflection tracker
5AI tools and mini-appsDecision matrices, structured data, HTML basics, prototype thinkingLEGO price estimator, e-bike comparison, simple web page
6Showcase weekStorytelling, demo, critique, iterationFinal demo: what we built, what we learned, what we would improve

Learning outcomes

  • Use AI to learn faster without outsourcing thinking.
  • Recognize when AI may be wrong or biased.
  • Build practical tools from prompts and simple pages.
  • Present a final project with confidence.

Daily rhythm

10 min demo · 25 min build · 15 min challenge · 5 min XP log.

Marketplace feel

The site looks outward-facing, but the first use case stays focused on the founding family cohort.
