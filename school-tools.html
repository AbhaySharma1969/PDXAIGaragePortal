Good AI school use

  • Ask for hints before answers.
  • Ask for a similar example.
  • Ask AI to quiz you.
  • Ask AI to explain your mistake.
  • Ask AI to make a study plan.

Bad AI school use

  • Copying answers without understanding.
  • Submitting AI writing as your own.
  • Skipping the thinking step.
  • Trusting every answer without checking.
  • Using AI to avoid practice.
