Coach prompt

Act as my beginner Spanish conversation partner. Scenario: [school / restaurant / sports / travel / friend]. Use Spanish 1–2 level language. Ask one question at a time in Spanish. After I answer, correct me gently in English and continue. At the end, create a polished version of the dialogue.

Session output

By the end of this session, create:

  • Artifact: Spanish roleplay script and corrected dialogue.
  • Proof: one screenshot, note, prompt, table, or mini-demo
  • Reflection: what worked, what failed, and what version 2 should improve
School Tools rule: Practice matters. The AI should help the student speak and revise, not skip the conversation.
