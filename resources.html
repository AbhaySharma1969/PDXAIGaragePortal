Responsible AI rules

  • AI is allowed to explain, quiz, brainstorm, organize, and critique.
  • AI is not allowed to replace Sahil’s own thinking for school submissions.
  • Claims must be checked before being treated as true.
  • Private or sensitive information should not be pasted into AI tools.
  • Every final artifact should include human judgment.
