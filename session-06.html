Investigation coach prompt

Act as my careful investigation coach. I am building a LEGO Case File. Help me sort these notes into verified facts, claims/allegations, opinions, and emotional language. For each item, ask: what evidence would confirm this? Do not make final conclusions.

Session output

By the end of this session, create:

  • Artifact: Evidence board with facts, claims, opinions, and proof needed.
  • Proof: one screenshot, note, table, or memo
  • Standard: label uncertainty and avoid unfair conclusions
Case File Rule

Do not let AI invent facts. Mark what is known, unknown, and needs checking.

Human Judgment Rule

A final conclusion must come from evidence, not from how confident AI sounds.
