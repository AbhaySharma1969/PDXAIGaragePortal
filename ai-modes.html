Lab 5

AI Modes: choose the right job for AI.

AI works better when Sahil tells it what role to play. The same topic can be handled by a tutor, critic, coach, planner, analyst, or builder.

Tutor

Teach me

Explains, gives examples, asks questions, and corrects mistakes.

Critic

Improve this

Finds weak spots, confusion, missing logic, and boring parts.

Coach

Train me

Creates practice plans, habit systems, and reflection questions.

Analyst

Organize evidence

Sorts facts, claims, opinions, risks, and tradeoffs.

Builder

Prototype this

Helps create pages, tools, checklists, and scorecards.

Editor

Make it clearer

Rewrites for clarity, tone, structure, and audience.

Mode switcher prompt

I want you to work in [Tutor/Critic/Coach/Analyst/Builder/Editor] mode. My goal is [goal]. My context is [context]. Your job is to [task]. Follow these rules: [rules]. Output format: [format].
