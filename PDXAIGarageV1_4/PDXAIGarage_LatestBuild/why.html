Why we exist

Teenagers already have AI. They need judgment.

PDX AI Garage began as a father-son summer project. The goal was not to teach another tool. The goal was to help a teenager become a more capable builder in an AI-powered world.

Not a prompt course

Builders learn how to verify, explain, and improve AI-assisted work.

Not school

We use missions, passports, artifacts, and reflections.

Not hype

AI is the medium. Human capability is the mission.

We are not building the biggest AI academy. We are building the one we wish had existed.
