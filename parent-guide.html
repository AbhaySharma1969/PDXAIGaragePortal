Mentor guide

How to support the student without taking over.

The mentor’s job is not to lecture. The job is to keep the student building, checking, and explaining their thinking.

Ask this after every mission

  • What did you build?
  • What did AI help with?
  • What did AI get wrong or make confusing?
  • What would version 2 improve?

Watch for these problems

  • Copying without understanding.
  • Using AI as an answer machine.
  • Skipping reflection.
  • Trusting facts without checking.

Good signs

  • The student can explain the artifact.
  • The student can name one AI weakness.
  • The student changes the prompt after testing.

Keep the pace simple

One mission per session is enough.
