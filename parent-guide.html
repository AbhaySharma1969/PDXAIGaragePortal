Parent / Mentor Guide

How to guide without taking over.

The parent role is to keep the student honest, curious, and building.

Ask after every mission

  • What did you build?
  • What did AI help with?
  • What did AI get wrong or make confusing?
  • What did you change yourself?
  • What would version 2 improve?

Watch for

  • Copying without understanding.
  • Skipping the quick check.
  • Trusting AI facts without checking.
  • Browsing pages instead of doing the mission.
