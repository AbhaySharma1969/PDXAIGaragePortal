Teaching Guide

How to teach the AI Garage curriculum.

Use this as a parent/mentor facilitation plan. The goal is not to lecture. The goal is to keep the student building, testing, explaining, and improving.

Recommended session format

5 min — Set the mission
Ask what the student will build today.
8 min — Learn + check
Read the AI Power-Up and answer the quick check.
12 min — Practice with AI
Copy the coach prompt, use ChatGPT, and answer its questions.
15–20 min — Build artifact
Create the actual output.
5 min — Reflect
What worked? What failed? What should version 2 improve?

Mentor questions

Can you explain the idea without reading the screen?What did AI help with?What did AI get wrong or make vague?What evidence or test did you use?What would you improve in version 2?

Rubric

1 — StartedStudent copied or read but cannot explain yet.
2 — PracticedStudent used AI and produced a rough artifact.
3 — BuilderStudent can explain, test, and improve the artifact.
4 — FounderStudent can teach the idea, show limits, and propose version 2.
