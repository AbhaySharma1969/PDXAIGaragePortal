Coach prompt

Act as my Spanish 1–2 vocabulary coach. Topic or word list: [paste words]. Quiz me one word at a time. Give a hint before the answer. Correct spelling and grammar gently. After 10 questions, show my weak words and give me a 10-minute practice plan.

Session output

By the end of this session, create:

  • Artifact: Spanish vocabulary coach prompt and one sample quiz.
  • Proof: one screenshot, note, prompt, table, or mini-demo
  • Reflection: what worked, what failed, and what version 2 should improve
School Tools rule: AI is allowed to quiz and coach. It should not simply give answers to copy.
